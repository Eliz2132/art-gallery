Starry Night

Artist

Vincent Van Gogh

Year of Creation

1889

Medium

Oil on Canvas

Description

"Starry Night" is one of the most iconic paintings of all time. This masterpiece depicts a swirling night sky over a small village, with vibrant blues and yellows creating a sense of movement and emotion. Van Gogh's bold brushstrokes and unique perspective transform a simple nighttime scene into a cosmic vision of beauty and wonder.
