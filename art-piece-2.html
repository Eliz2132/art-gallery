The Persistence of Memory

Artist

Salvador Dalí

Year of Creation

1931

Medium

Oil on Canvas

Description

"The Persistence of Memory" is a surrealist masterpiece featuring melting clocks draped across a barren landscape. Dalí's dreamlike imagery challenges our perception of time and reality. The soft, drooping watches symbolize the irrelevance of time in the subconscious mind, creating one of the most recognizable and thought-provoking works in modern art.
