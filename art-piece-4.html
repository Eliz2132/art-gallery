Water Lilies

Artist

Claude Monet

Year of Creation

1906

Medium

Oil on Canvas

Description

"Water Lilies" is a masterpiece of impressionist painting that captures the serene beauty of a Japanese bridge garden. Monet's delicate brushwork and soft color palette create a tranquil atmosphere, with floating water lilies and their reflections merging with the water below. This series of paintings exemplifies the artist's obsession with light and its effects on nature.
